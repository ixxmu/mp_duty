Ro/e算法介绍</h3><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器">张泽民老师团队的文章基本上都用的是<code>Ro/e</code>算法，<code>Ro/e</code>是观察到的细胞数与期望细胞数的比值，用于量化每个亚群对组织的偏好程度，例如《Lineage tracking reveals dynamic relationships of T cells in colorectal cancer》<strong>PMID: 30479382</strong>的Fig 1b，以及髓细胞图谱《A pan-cancer single-cell transcriptional atlas of tumor infiltrating myeloid cells》<strong>PMID: 33545035</strong> ：</p><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器"><strong>PMID: 30479382</strong>的Fig 1b的图例是</p><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器">Tissue preference of each cluster estimated by the STARTRAC-dist index. +++, <em>R**o/e</em> &gt; 1; ++, 0.8 &lt; <em>R**o/e</em> ≤ 1; +, 0.2 ≤ <em>R**o/e</em> ≤ 0.8; +/−, 0 &lt; <em>R**o/e</em> &lt; 0.2; −, <em>R**o/e</em> = 0; in which <em>R**o/e</em> denotes the ratio of observed to expected cell number.
