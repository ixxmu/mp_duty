leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 是维持免疫耐受、调控免疫稳态的关键 CD4⁺T 细胞亚群。但人类 Treg 高度异质，</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">单一标记（如 FoxP3）不足以定义所有功能性 Treg</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，必须从</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">表型、起源、功能、克隆、疾病、表观、翻译后修饰</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">多维度综合界定。</span></span></p><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">一、分子表型维度（最常用，但非绝对）</span></span></h2><h2><span leaf=""><br></span></h2><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">经典 Treg 表型：</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">CD4⁺CD25⁺CD127⁻FoxP3⁺</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，但人类中存在大量</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">FoxP3⁻功能性 Treg</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">和</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">FoxP3⁺无抑制功能的效应 T 细胞（Teff）</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">。</span></span></p><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">其他候选标记：</span></span></p><ul><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">CTLA-4、GITR、LAG-3、TIGIT、CD39/CD73</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">与抑制功能相关，但激活 Teff 也表达。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Helios、Nrp-1</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">用于区分胸腺 Treg（tTreg）与外周诱导 Treg（pTreg），但人类特异性有限。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">GARP、LAP、GPA33</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">部分 Treg 表达，但非特异性。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">TSDR（Treg 特异性去甲基化区域）</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">FoxP3 基因区</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">稳定去甲基化</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，是</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">稳定 Treg</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">的金标准，可区分瞬时 FoxP3⁺Teff。</span></span></section></li></ul><p><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">表型只能 “富集” Treg，不能唯一确定功能性 Treg。</span></span></strong></p><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">二、起源维度（tTreg、pTreg、iTreg）</span></span></h2><h2><span leaf=""><br></span></h2><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">按发育与诱导来源，Treg 分为三类：</span></span></p><ul><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">胸腺 Treg（tTreg/nTreg）</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">胸腺中高亲和力自身抗原诱导，</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Helios⁺Nrp-1⁺</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，主要维持中枢耐受，识别自身抗原。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">外周诱导 Treg（pTreg）</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">外周淋巴 / 组织由 TGF-β、维 A 酸诱导，</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Helios⁻Nrp-1⁻</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，识别外来 / 食物 / 共生抗原。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">体外诱导 Treg（iTreg）</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">体外 TGF-β 诱导，稳定性差，</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Tr1、Th3</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">属于功能性 iTreg。</span></span></section></li></ul><p><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">起源≠功能，同一功能可来自不同起源。</span></span></strong></p><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">三、功能维度（金标准）</span></span></h2><p><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">只有抑制功能能真正定义 Treg</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，与表型无关。</span></span></p><ul><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">抑制抗原呈递</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">CTLA-4 竞争 CD80/86、LAG-3 结合 MHC-II、诱导 DC 分泌 IDO。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">分泌抑制因子</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">IL-10、TGF-β、IL-35。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">代谢抑制</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">CD39/CD73 产腺苷、耗竭 IL-2。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">直接杀伤</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">颗粒酶 B、穿孔素、FasL 诱导 Teff 凋亡。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">感染性耐受</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 改造 DC 为耐受型，诱导新 Treg。</span></span></section></li></ul><ul><li><section><span leaf=""><br></span></section></li></ul><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">四、克隆与异质性维度</span></span></h2><h2><span leaf=""><br></span></h2><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">即使同一抗原特异性 Treg 克隆，</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">功能也高度异质</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">：</span></span></p><ul><li><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">克隆 1：高 IL-10、强单核细胞杀伤、抑制初始 T 与 Th1。</span></span></section></li><li><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">克隆 2：以颗粒酶 B 为主，强单核细胞杀伤。</span></span></section></li><li><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">克隆 3：强抑制、可诱导感染性耐受。</span></span></section></li><li><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">...</span></span></section></li></ul><p><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 不是单一群体，而是功能各异的克隆集合。</span></span></strong></p><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">五、疾病与微环境维度</span></span></h2><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 在不同疾病中表型、功能、稳定性差异巨大</span></span></p><ul><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">自身免疫病</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 数量正常但</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">功能缺陷</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">，FoxP3 稳定性下降、乙酰化降低。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">肿瘤微环境</span></span></strong></li></ul><ul><li><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 大量浸润、功能亢进，高 CTLA-4/PD-1，促进免疫逃逸。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">感染 / 移植</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 抑制过强或过弱，决定免疫平衡。</span></span></section></li><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">组织特异性</span></span></strong><section><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">肠道、皮肤、肿瘤浸润 Treg 表型、功能各不相同。</span></span></section></li></ul><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">结论：</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">Treg 定义必须结合疾病与微环境背景。</span></span></strong></p><h2><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">六、表观调控维度</span></span></h2><p><span leaf=""><br></span></p><p><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">FoxP3 表达与稳定性由</span></span><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">表观修饰</span></span></strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">决定：</span></span></p><ul><li><strong><span leaf=""><span textstyle="">TSDR 去甲基化</span></span></strong><section><span
