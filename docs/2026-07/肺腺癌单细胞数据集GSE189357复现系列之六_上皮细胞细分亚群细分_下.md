data-nest-level="4"><br></span></p><h2 data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span data-nest-level="4"></span><span data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">前言</span></span><span data-nest-level="4"></span></h2><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">Hello小伙伴们大家好，我是生信技能树的罗小黑。今天是肺腺癌单细胞数据集GSE189357复现系列第六期 下。上周我们我们提取了上皮细胞亚群，再次走标准的Seurat V5流程。本期，我们将对结合拷贝数变异inferCNV分析对上皮细胞进行亚群细分。</span></p><h3 data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span data-nest-level="4"></span><span data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">1.背景介绍</span></span><span data-nest-level="4"></span></h3><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">上一期我们将上皮细胞细分为了0-8九个亚群。我们知道，肺腺癌正常肺泡上皮（AT1/AT2）、气道上皮细胞有稳态特征基因；恶性上皮细胞会特异性高表达肿瘤驱动、上皮间质转化、肿瘤干性、腺癌特征致癌 Marker，基于 Seurat/scanpy 表达量、FeaturePlot、DotPlot、VlnPlot 可快速初筛疑似恶性上皮亚群。</span></p><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">但是，仅靠 Marker 鉴定肿瘤细胞存在多重缺陷：</span></p><blockquote><span data-nest-level="4"></span><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">1.假阳性：炎症修复、气道固有上皮会异常表达肿瘤标志物，误判良性细胞；</span></p><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">2.假阴性：EMT、低分化、TTF1 阴性、休眠肿瘤细胞会丢失特征 Marker 造成漏检；</span></p><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">3.无基因组证据：转录表达无法区分增生上皮与真正恶变细胞；</span></p><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">4.技术干扰：双联体细胞、低质量细胞、测序批次会产生表达假象；</span></p><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">5.Marker 非特异：间质相关基因在基质细胞中本底表达，易混淆；</span></p><p data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">6.无法鉴别肺原发腺癌与其他器官转移癌。</span></p></blockquote><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">如何进一步区分上皮细胞亚群的良恶性？在这里，我们使用了金标准，拷贝数变异（CNV）分析方法--inferCNV。inferCNV 是 Broad研究所开发的R包，只用单细胞转录组（scRNA-seq）表达数据，间接推断单细胞染色体拷贝数变异CNV，不用额外单细胞DNA测序。</span></p><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">其核心原理是基因剂量效应：染色体片段扩增→该区基因整体表达偏高；片段缺失→表达整体偏低。以正常细胞（免疫/基质）做表达基线，沿染色体滑动窗口平滑降噪，用HMM模型划分缺失/正常/扩增三种拷贝数状态。</span></p><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">核心用途包括：</span></p><ol><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">区分肿瘤恶性细胞与基质、免疫正常细胞</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">解析肿瘤内部遗传异质性，划分亚克隆</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">定位扩增/缺失染色体区段，关联驱动突变、肿瘤演化</span></section></li></ol><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">优缺点：</span></p><ol><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">优点：依托常规scRNA数据，成本低、单细胞分辨率、可视化直观；</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">局限：仅推断大片段CNV，微小拷贝改变分辨差，无法精确拷贝数定量。</span></section></li></ol><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">主要分析流程如下：</span></p><ol><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">输入：表达矩阵、基因染色体位置、正常参考细胞标注</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">标准化校正测序偏差</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">与正常基线对比，染色体滑动平均降噪</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">HMM预测CNV区段，绘制全基因组CNV热图</span></section></li><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="6">按CNV模式聚类，区分恶性/正常细胞、肿瘤亚克隆</span></section></li></ol><h3 data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span data-nest-level="4"></span><span data-nest-level="4"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="5">2.拷贝数变异（CNV）分析流程</span></span><span data-nest-level="4"></span></h3><p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" data-nest-level="3"><span leaf="" data-nest-level="4">开始分析之前，需要准备三个输入文件：</span></p><ol><li><section data-nest-level="5"><span leaf=""
