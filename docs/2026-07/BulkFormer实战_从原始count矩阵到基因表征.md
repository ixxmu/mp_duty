leaf=""> 或数据资源不匹配。重新下载同一发布版本的代码、权重和 Zenodo 文件。</span></p><p><strong><span leaf="">报错二：CUDA out of memory</span></strong></p><p><span leaf="">先把 </span><code><span leaf="">batch_size</span></code><span leaf=""> 和自动混合精度。</span></p><p><strong><span leaf="">报错三：mask probability 异常高</span></strong></p><p><span leaf="">检查基因 ID 类型、Ensembl 版本号、物种和重复列。BulkFormer 当前是人类模型，不能直接输入 mm10
